The Survivor

published 1976

verdict: pretty good

  • A plane crashes into Eton and the Standard James Herbert Protagonist, who mysteriously survived the crash, must work out what happened and whether it was his fault.

  • Some good horror set-pieces loosely threaded together. I read the book a few weeks ago and I can't remember why it was all happening. Something about industrial espionage and occultism. I do remember a section where it was deemed impossible to bring down a passenger jet these days without it being an inside job because of security being impenetrable, which was interesting to read in the post-9/11 world.
