Medusas's Children

published 1977

verdict: good!

  • just what one is looking for in an old paperback scifi book.
  • descendents of a ships crew are marooned in a sphere of water in space, eking out an existence in the extreme conditions.
  • this happened because of global warming causing sea levels to rise. people used wormholes to suck the extra water into space. some people got sucked in by accident.
  • I think the name refers to the raft of the medusa? Can't remember if this is mentioned in the text.
